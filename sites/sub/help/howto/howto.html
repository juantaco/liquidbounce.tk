How to Install

Step 1: Download LiquidBounce from LiquidBounce.tk and unpack it to your desktop.

Step 2: Press Win + R on you keyboard and open %AppData%. Than naviagate to ".minecraft".

Step 3: Open "versions" and drag LiquidBounce into it.

Start Minecraft, press on "New Profile" and choose "LiquidBounce" from the list of available minecraft versions

Hit "Play" to start LiquidBounce. Make sure to choose the right profile next time you want to use LiquidBounce.
